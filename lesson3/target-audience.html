Audience Description

The target audience for this website are local businesses, local citizens, tourists, and new businesses

Persona 1: Business Owner

Philip owns a small shop downtown. He wants to use the Chamber of Commerce website to find information on community events to better market his business. He also hopes to find contact info for other business owners to coordinate events and network.

Persona 2: Local

Jane recently moved to the area. She and her husband have 3 young children. She is using the Chamber of Commerce website to find activities that she and her family can attend that are child friendly.

Scenarios

  • What community events are there?
  • What kinds of outdoor activities are nearby?
  • Are there any historical landmarks?
  • How does the Chamber of Commerce help businesses?
  • How friendly is Tallassee to small businesses?
